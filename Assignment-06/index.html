Quick Protien Packed pancakes

Ingredients

Pancakes:

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup plain Greek yougart
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup + 1 tbsp oat flower
  • 1 scoop 30 g whey protein powder

Directions

  1. Combine all ingreadients into a large bowl.
  2. Stir the contents in bowl to a thick liquid form.
  3. Heat up a medium size pan, then apply butter or non stick cooking spray
  4. Pour pancake batter into pan forming small circles, note the pours should be no bigger than CD, this will ensure for even cooking throughout.
  5. Once the pancakes start to bubble on the top flip and continue cooking for 2 minutes.
  6. When you have cooked all of the batter, plate the cakes and serve with peanut butter, whipped cream, and maple syrup.
